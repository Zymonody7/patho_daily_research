## 摘要
引言：微生物组已成为肺癌肿瘤生物学和免疫反应的关键调节因子。肺部曾被认为是无菌的，而现在被公认为拥有多样化的微生物组，其通过肠-肺轴相互作用来塑造炎症、免疫逃逸和治疗结果。涵盖领域：本研究回顾了将微生物组组成和功能与肺癌发展及免疫检查点抑制剂（ICIs）反应联系起来的现有证据。我们探讨了微生物影响宿主免疫的机制，确定了与治疗结果相关的关键分类群，并总结了治疗策略，如粪便微生物群移植、益生菌、后生元、饮食改变、抗生素和工程化活体生物治疗药物。文献检索在多个数据库和来源中进行，包括 PubMed、Scopus、Embase 和临床试验注册库，截至 2025 年 8 月，重点关注与肺癌、免疫疗法和微生物组靶向干预相关的临床前和临床研究。专家意见：微生物组研究通过提供新的生物标志物和治疗靶点，正在重新定义精准肿瘤学。尽管早期临床试验显示出提高 ICI 疗效的潜力，但其实施受到供体变异性、方法学差异和生物安全问题的限制。标准化方案、机制研究和生物标志物驱动的患者选择对于将微生物组调节纳入常规肺癌治疗至关重要。

## Abstract
INTRODUCTION: The microbiome has emerged as a critical regulator of tumor biology and immune response in lung cancer. Once considered sterile, the lung is now recognized to harbor a diverse microbiome that interacts with the gut - lung axis to shape inflammation, immune evasion, and therapeutic outcomes. AREAS COVERED: This study reviews current evidence linking microbiome composition and function to lung cancer development and response to immune checkpoint inhibitors (ICIs). We explore mechanisms of microbial influence on host immunity, identify key taxa associated with treatment outcomes, and summarize therapeutic strategies such as fecal microbiota transplantation, probiotics, postbiotics, dietary changes, antibiotics, and engineered live biotherapeutics. The literature search was performed across multiple databases and sources, including PubMed, Scopus, Embase, and clinical trial registries, up to August 2025, focusing on both preclinical and clinical studies related to lung cancer, immunotherapy, and microbiome-targeted interventions. EXPERT OPINION: Microbiome research is redefining precision oncology by presenting new biomarkers and therapeutic targets. Although early-phase trials show potential to improve ICI efficacy, implementation is limited by donor variability, methodological differences, and biosafety issues. Standardized protocols, mechanistic studies, and biomarker-driven patient selection will be crucial to incorporating microbiome modulation into routine lung cancer treatment.
