## 摘要
抗菌药物耐药性持续限制了治疗细菌感染的有效药物数量。开发具有独特作用机制的抗菌药物对于应对这一威胁至关重要。通过采用人工智能、全细胞筛选和药物重利用等多种先导化合物识别方法，可以鉴定出对细菌病原体具有活性的小分子。本文介绍了利用这些药物发现策略发现的四种分子（abaucin、enterololin、lolamicin 和 fendiline），它们对大肠杆菌（Escherichia coli）或鲍曼不动杆菌（Acinetobacter baumannii）表现出高效力。研究发现这些分子均靶向脂蛋白转运途径（Lol）。在革兰氏阴性菌中，脂蛋白对细胞生存至关重要，这使得 Lol 途径成为开发抗菌药物的一个极具吸引力的靶点。本综述强调了脂蛋白作为抗菌靶点的重要性，并详细介绍了 Lol 小分子抑制剂开发的四个实例。

## Abstract
Antimicrobial resistance continues to limit the number of effective antimicrobials to treat bacterial infections. The development of antimicrobials with unique mechanisms of action is crucial to overcoming this threat. By employing various methods of hit identification, such as artificial intelligence, whole-cell screening, and drug repurposing, small-molecules that display activity against bacterial pathogens can be identified. Herein, four molecules (abaucin, enterololin, lolamicin, and fendiline) that were found to have high potency against either Escherichia coli or Acinetobacter baumannii utilizing these drug discovery strategies are described. They were all found to target the lipoprotein trafficking pathway (Lol). Within Gram-negative species, lipoproteins are essential for cell viability, making the Lol pathway an interesting target to exploit for the discovery of antimicrobials. This review highlights the importance of lipoproteins as antibacterial targets and details four examples of the development of small-molecule inhibitors of Lol.
