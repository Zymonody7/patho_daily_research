Abstract
--------

We report 13 metagenome-assembled genomes (MAGs) of _Paraglaciecola chathamensis_ (_Gammaproteobacteria_) retrieved from farmed Atlantic Nori (_Porphyra_ spp.) across several developmental stages. MAGs encode proteins involved in host-microbe interactions, nutrient acquisition, nitrogen and cofactor metabolism, stress resilience, and genome plasticity, illuminating the possible roles of _Paraglaciecola_ in the _Porphyra_ holobiont.

**Keywords:** Atlantic Nori; Paraglaciecola; Porphyra; algal microbiomes; aquaculture; bacterial genomics; host-microbe interactions; marine symbioses.
