## 摘要
背景：胶质瘤是成人中枢神经系统最常见的恶性肿瘤。低级别胶质瘤（LGG）具有级别进展和组织学转化的潜力，而这种恶性演化的相关机制尚不明确。方法：在本研究中，我们利用来自28名患者的61份原发-复发配对胶质瘤样本，基于DIA-NN方法进行了蛋白质组学分析。结果：我们的结果表明，Ras/p38-MAPK通路是驱动LGG级别进展和组织学转化的核心信号通路。由复制因子C（RFC）介导的非MGMT依赖性非特异性DNA损伤修复系统的激活可能是导致治疗耐药的重要原因。此外，代谢重编程广泛参与了LGG级别进展和组织学转化的调节。不饱和脂肪酸合成的增强以及过氧化物酶体脂肪酸β-氧化通路的显著激活是LGG组织学转化后的代谢特征。最后，我们构建了一个由2种蛋白质组成的可靠LGG进展预测模型，可作为LGG进展的监测生物标志物，具有潜在的临床转化价值。结论：综上所述，胶质瘤级别进展和组织学转化是复杂的生物学过程，涉及Ras/p38-MAPK信号通路的异常上调、由RFC复合体调节的增强型非MGMT依赖性非特异性DNA损伤修复系统，以及基于HK1-PFKP-ENO2介导的糖酵解和过氧化物酶体脂肪酸β-氧化的代谢重编程。本研究填补了LGG级别进展和组织学转化相关研究的空白，为胶质瘤进展的生物学机制提供了新颖且独特的见解。

## Abstract
BACKGROUND: Glioma was the most common malignant tumor of the central nervous system in adults. Low-grade gliomas (LGGs) have a potential of grade progression and histological transformation, and the relevant mechanisms of this malignant evolution were still unclear. METHODS: In this study, we used 61 primary-recurrent paired glioma samples from 28 patients for proteomics analysis based upon DIA-NN approach. RESULTS: Our results indicated that the Ras/p38-MAPK pathways were hub signaling pathways driving grade progression and histological transformation in LGG. The activation of non-MGMT dependent unspecific DNA damage repair system mediated by Replication Factor C (RFC) can be an important reason for the treatment resistance. Moreover, metabolic reprogramming was widely involved in the regulation of LGG grade progression and histological transformation. The enhanced synthesis of unsaturated fatty acids and the significantly activated peroxisomal fatty acid beta-oxidation pathways were metabolic characteristics of LGG after histological transformation. Last, we constructed a reliable LGG progression prediction model consisted of 2 proteins, which can be monitor biomarkers for LGG progression, with potential clinical translation value. CONCLUSIONS: In summary, glioma grade progression and histological transformation were complex biological processes, which involved the abnormal up-regulation of Ras/p38-MAPK signalings, enhanced non-MGMT dependent unspecific DNA damage repair system regulated by RFC complex and metabolic reprogramming based on HK1-PFKP-ENO2 mediated glycolysis and peroxisomal FA beta oxidation. This study filled the gap in relevant research on the grade progression and histological transformation of LGG, providing novel and unique insights into the biological mechanisms of glioma progression.
