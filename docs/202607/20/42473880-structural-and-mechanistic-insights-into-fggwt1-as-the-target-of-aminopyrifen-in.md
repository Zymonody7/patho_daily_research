## 摘要
由禾谷镰刀菌（Fusarium graminearum）引起的小麦赤霉病（FHB）是一种全球性的毁灭性小麦病害。目前的杀菌剂面临多种局限性，包括药效不稳定和抗药性风险。在本研究中，我们利用分子动力学模拟、定点诱变和田间试验，剖析了氨基吡啶芬（aminopyrifen）与其靶标 FgGWT1 之间的相互作用。FgGWT1 是一种内质网驻留蛋白，对生长、繁殖和致病性至关重要。敲除 FgGWT1 意外地会上调 TRI 基因的表达和脱氧雪腐镰刀菌烯醇（DON）的积累，揭示了其在霉菌毒素生物合成中的负调节作用。同源建模和分子动力学模拟进一步确定了五个潜在的结合口袋。这些口袋内关键残基的诱变降低了对氨基吡啶芬的敏感性并减弱了致病性，提供了功能性证据。此外，田间评估证明了氨基吡啶芬的实际应用潜力。这项工作建立了 FgGWT1 抑制的结构框架，识别了控制抑制剂结合和毒力的残基，并为针对 GPI 锚定途径的下一代杀菌剂开发提供了机制见解。

## Abstract
Fusarium head blight (FHB), caused by Fusarium graminearum, is a globally devastating wheat disease. Currently fungicides face various limitations, including inconsistent effectiveness and resistance risks. Here, we dissect the interaction between aminopyrifen and its target FgGWT1 using molecular dynamics simulation, site-directed mutagenesis, and field trials. FgGWT1 is an ER-resident protein essential for growth, reproduction, and pathogenicity. Deletion of FgGWT1 unexpectedly upregulates TRI gene expression and DON accumulation, revealing a negative regulatory role in mycotoxin biosynthesis. Homology modeling and molecular dynamics simulations further identify five potential binding pockets. Mutagenesis of key residues within these pockets reduces aminopyrifen sensitivity and attenuates pathogenicity, providing functional evidence. Moreover, field evaluations demonstrated the practical potential of aminopyrifen. This work establishes a structural framework for FgGWT1 inhibition, identifies residues governing inhibitor binding and virulence, and provides mechanistic insights for next-generation fungicide development targeting the GPI anchor pathway.
