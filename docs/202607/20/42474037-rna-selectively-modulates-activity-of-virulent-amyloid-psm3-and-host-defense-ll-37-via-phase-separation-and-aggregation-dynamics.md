## 摘要
形成淀粉样蛋白的肽类正日益被认为是宿主-病原体界面上的动态调节因子，然而环境因素如何控制它们的组装和活性仍不清楚。在此，RNA 作为一种浓度依赖性调节因子，作用于两种序列相关但组装行为根本不同的 α-螺旋肽：形成 cross-α 淀粉样蛋白的金黄色葡萄球菌毒力因子 PSMα3 和非淀粉样蛋白形成的人类宿主防御肽 LL-37。RNA 驱动 PSMα3 经历不同的组装状态，从液态凝聚物到纤维状多态体，同时随时间推移保留其细胞毒性和抗菌活性。相比之下，RNA 减弱了 LL-37 对宿主细胞的细胞毒性，同时维持了其抗菌活性，这与宿主保护性的免疫调节作用一致。结合表没食子儿茶素没食子酸酯（EGCG）的相反作用（它将这两种肽重定向为无定形组装体），这些发现支持了一个机制模型，即生物活性是由超分子结构、组装轨迹和动力学决定的，而非仅仅取决于单体丰度或成熟纤维。更广泛地说，我们的研究结果确定了 RNA 是 α-螺旋肽组装的环境调节因子，并将组装状态控制确立为毒力和宿主防御的可调控决定因素。

## Abstract
Amyloid-forming peptides are increasingly recognized as dynamic regulators at the host-pathogen interface, yet how environmental factors control their assembly and activity remains poorly understood. Here, RNA acts as a concentration-dependent regulator of two sequence-related α-helical peptides with fundamentally different assembly behaviors: the cross-α amyloid-forming Staphylococcus aureus virulence factor PSMα3 and the non-amyloidogenic human host-defense peptide LL-37. RNA drives PSMα3 through distinct assembly states, from liquid-like condensates to fibrillar polymorphs, while preserving cytotoxic and antimicrobial activity over time. In contrast, RNA attenuates LL-37 cytotoxicity toward host cells while maintaining antibacterial activity, consistent with a host-protective immunomodulatory effect. Together with the opposing effects of epigallocatechin gallate, which redirects both peptides into amorphous assemblies, these findings support a mechanistic model in which biological activity is governed by supramolecular architecture, assembly trajectory, and dynamics rather than by monomer abundance or mature fibrils alone. More broadly, our findings identify RNA as an environmental regulator of α-helical peptide assemblies, and establish assembly-state control as a tunable determinant of virulence and host defense.
