## 摘要
背景：慢性同种异体肺移植物失能（CLAD）是肺移植后长期生存的主要障碍。肺移植物微生物组的组成已被作为移植物存活的预后标志物进行探索，但大多数研究测量的是细菌的相对丰度而非绝对丰度。细菌丰度的绝对定量能够改善对不同疾病状态和生理部位中宿主-微生物相互作用的评估，但尚未与单纯的组成指标在识别 CLAD 相关移植物微生物组的表现方面进行过比较。方法：我们进行了一项病例对照研究，比较了移植后前 4 年内发生 CLAD 的肺移植受者（病例组）与移植后前 5 年内未发生 CLAD 的受者（对照组）之间肺移植物微生物组的组成。在移植后 12 个月内，收集了 53 例病例和 55 例对照的支气管肺泡灌洗液，以比较微生物组的组成特征和细菌绝对丰度。结果：我们鉴定了肺移植物微生物组的 5 种重叠组成亚型，称为群落状态类型（CST），分别由普雷沃氏菌属（Prevotella）、链球菌属（Streptococcus）和奈瑟菌属（Neisseria）、葡萄球菌属（Staphylococcus）或假单胞菌属（Pseudomonas）主导，或由多样化的细菌群落组成。在分类学多样化的群落中观察到较低的细菌密度，且对照组的细菌密度低于病例组。将定义 CST 的细菌相对丰度根据总细菌密度进行归一化处理，增强了微生物组组成与慢性同种异体肺移植物失能（CLAD）之间的关联。结论：细菌绝对定量加强了肺移植后肺部微生物组与 CLAD 之间的关联，并可能增进我们对肺部微生物组如何影响肺移植预后的理解。

## Abstract
BACKGROUND: Chronic lung allograft dysfunction (CLAD) is a barrier to long-term survival following lung transplantation. Composition of the lung allograft microbiome has been explored as a prognostic marker for allograft survival, but most studies have measured bacterial relative, not absolute, abundances. Absolute quantitation of bacterial abundances improves the assessment of host-microbe interactions in diverse disease states and physiologic compartments but has not been compared to compositional indices alone for performance in identifying CLAD-associated allograft microbiomes. METHODS: We performed a case-control study to compare the composition of the lung allograft microbiome between lung transplant recipients who developed CLAD within the first 4 years of transplant (cases) versus those who remained CLAD-free for the first 5 years post-transplant (controls). Within 12 months post-transplantation, bronchoalveolar lavage fluid was collected from 53 cases and 55 controls to compare compositional features of the microbiome and bacterial absolute abundance. RESULTS: We identified 5 overlapping compositional subtypes, termed community state types (CST), of the lung allograft microbiome that were dominated by either Prevotella species, Streptococcus and Neisseria species, Staphylococcus or Pseudomonas species, or a diverse bacterial population. Lower bacterial density was observed in the taxonomically diverse community and was lower in controls than cases. Normalizing the relative abundance of CST-defining bacteria to total bacterial density strengthened microbiome-composition/chronic lung allograft dysfunction (CLAD) associations. CONCLUSIONS: Absolute bacterial quantification strengthens associations between the lung microbiome and CLAD following lung transplantation and may improve our understanding of how the lung microbiome affects lung transplant outcomes.
