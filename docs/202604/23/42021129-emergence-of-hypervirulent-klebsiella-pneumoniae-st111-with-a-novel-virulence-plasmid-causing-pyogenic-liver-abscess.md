## 摘要
目的：高毒力肺炎克雷伯菌（hvKp）相关的化脓性肝脓肿最常与 K1/K2 荚膜血清型以及 ST23、ST65 和 ST86 等典型谱系相关。这些谱系之外的 hvKp 报道仍然罕见，但具有重要的公共卫生意义。我们研究了一例由序列分型 111（ST111）型 hvKp 引起的一名免疫功能正常患者的罕见肝脓肿病例。方法：通过抗菌药物敏感性试验、全基因组测序和脉冲场凝胶电泳，对从一名化脓性肝脓肿患者中回收的五株分离株（KP1-KP5）进行了表征。为了研究毒力质粒对宿主菌株的贡献，进行了 CRISPR/Cas9 介导的质粒消除，随后进行了包括表型表征和鼠感染模型在内的功能分析。此外，还进行了比较基因组分析，以阐明高毒力的流行病学关系和遗传决定因素。结果：从患者中回收的五株分离株在遗传上是相同的。基因组分析鉴定出一个 181 kb 的 IncHI1B/IncFIB 毒力质粒，该质粒编码 rmpADC 和沙门菌素基因簇（iroBCDN），但缺失 iuc 和 rmpA2。值得注意的是，该质粒还携带耶尔森菌素位点（ybt），该位点通常起源于染色体。比较基因组学显示，类似的 ST111 菌株仅在中国有报道，这表明一个先前未被识别的 hvKp 谱系发生了克隆扩张。功能实验表明，质粒消除消除了高黏性表型，减少了荚膜产量，并完全减弱了小鼠模型中的毒力，证实该质粒是高毒力的主要驱动因素。有趣的是，质粒丢失增强了生物膜的形成并减轻了生长负担，表明与毒力相关的适应性权衡。结论：本研究描述了一例罕见的 hvKp ST111 肝脓肿病例，并鉴定出一种赋予高毒力的新型质粒。这些发现拓宽了已知的 hvKp 多样性，并强调了非典型谱系可以获得能够引起严重疾病的强效毒力决定因素。持续的临床和基因组监测对于在大范围传播前检测和遏制新兴的 hvKp 克隆至关重要。

## Abstract
OBJECTIVE: Hypervirulent Klebsiella pneumoniae (hvKp)-associated pyogenic liver abscess is most commonly linked to K1/K2 capsule serotypes and canonical lineages such as ST23, ST65, and ST86. Reports of hvKp outside these lineages remain rare but carry significant public health implications. We investigated an unusual case of liver abscess in an immunocompetent patient caused by hvKp sequence type 111 (ST111). METHODS: Five isolates (KP1-KP5) recovered from a patient with pyogenic liver abscess were characterized by antimicrobial susceptibility testing, whole-genome sequencing, and pulsed-field gel electrophoresis. To investigate the contribution of the virulence plasmid to the host strain, CRISPR/Cas9-mediated plasmid curing was performed, followed by functional analyses including phenotypic characterization and a murine infection model. In addition, comparative genomic analyses were conducted to elucidate the epidemiological relationships and genetic determinants underlying hypervirulence. RESULTS: The five isolates recovered from the patient were genetically identical. Genomic analysis identified a 181 kb IncHI1B/IncFIB virulence plasmid encoding rmpADC and the salmochelin cluster (iroBCDN), but lacking iuc and rmpA2. Remarkably, the plasmid also carried the yersiniabactin locus (ybt), typically chromosomal in origin. Comparative genomics revealed that similar ST111 strains have been reported exclusively in China, suggesting clonal expansion of a previously unrecognized hvKp lineage. Functional assays demonstrated that plasmid curing abolished the hypermucoviscous phenotype, reduced capsule production, and completely attenuated virulence in murine models, confirming the plasmid as the principal driver of hypervirulence. Interestingly, plasmid loss enhanced biofilm formation and alleviated growth burden, indicating fitness trade-offs associated with virulence. CONCLUSION: This study describes a rare case of hvKp ST111 liver abscess and identifies a novel plasmid conferring hypervirulence. These findings broaden the recognized diversity of hvKp and highlight that non-canonical lineages can acquire potent virulence determinants capable of causing severe disease. Ongoing clinical and genomic surveillance is essential to detect and contain emerging hvKp clones before widespread dissemination.
