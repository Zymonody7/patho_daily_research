## 摘要
背景：牛子宫炎症会损害繁殖力、动物福利和经济效益。白细胞介素-1 (IL-1) 家族蛋白的平衡调节对于消除子宫内膜炎症至关重要。目的：本研究旨在检测牛子宫内膜外植体中白细胞介素 1B (IL1B) 及其受体拮抗剂 (IL1RA) 的 mRNA 表达。方法：将来自 26 头母牛的子宫内膜外植体与热灭活的致病性大肠杆菌 (Escherichia coli)、机会性短小芽孢杆菌 (Bacillus pumilus) 或共生且具有潜在保护作用的布氏乳杆菌 (Lactobacillus buchneri) 共同孵育（3、14 和 24 小时），并与细胞因子（24 小时；IL1B、IL17A、IL10）共同孵育。通过反转录定量聚合酶链反应 (RT-qPCR) 定量 IL1B 和 IL1RA 的 mRNA 表达，并使用混合效应模型进行统计分析。主要结果：在所有时间点，细菌挑战后 IL1B mRNA 表达均显著增加 (P < 0.001)。大肠杆菌和短小芽孢杆菌暴露后，IL1RA mRNA 表达仅在 3 小时增加，而布氏乳杆菌暴露后在 14 和 24 小时增加 (P < 0.010)。在所有时间点和所有细菌挑战中，均检测到 IL1RA:IL1B 比率向促炎方向转变 (P < 0.020)。IL10 刺激降低了 IL1B 和 IL1RA mRNA 的表达，并使 IL1RA:IL1B 比率向抗炎方向转变 (P < 0.001)。结论：本研究证实外植体培养是分析牛子宫内膜炎症调节的合适模型，并确定 IL10 是子宫内膜 IL1B 和 IL1RA 的强效调节因子。意义：不仅是大肠杆菌和短小芽孢杆菌，出乎意料的是，布氏乳杆菌也诱导了初始促炎 mRNA 表达模式，且该模式随培养时间而异。这突显了有必要针对致病菌、机会致病菌与潜在保护性细菌在牛子宫健康中的作用开展进一步研究。

## Abstract
CONTEXT: Bovine uterine inflammation impairs fertility, animal welfare and economics. Balanced regulation of interleukin-1 family proteins is crucial to resolve endometrial inflammation. AIM: mRNA expression of interleukin 1B (IL1B) and its receptor antagonist (IL1RA) was examined in bovine endometrial explants. METHODS: Endometrial explants from 26 cows were incubated with heat-inactivated pathogenic Escherichia coli, opportunistic Bacillus pumilus or commensal and potentially protective Lactobacillus buchneri (3, 14 and 24 h) and with cytokines (24 h; IL1B, IL17A, IL10). mRNA expression of IL1B and IL1RA was quantified via reverse-transcription quantitative polymerase chain reaction (RT-qPCR) and mixed effects models were used for statistical analysis. KEY RESULTS: IL1B mRNA expression increased after bacterial challenge at all timepoints (P < 0.001). IL1RA mRNA expression increased after Escherichia coli and Bacillus pumilus exposure only at 3 h, but after Lactobacillus buchneri at 14 and 24 h (P < 0.010). A pro-inflammatory shift of the IL1RA:IL1B ratio was detected at all timepoints and for all bacterial challenges (P < 0.020). Stimulation with IL10 reduced IL1B and IL1RA mRNA expression and shifted the IL1RA:IL1B ratio in an anti-inflammatory direction (P < 0.001). CONCLUSION: This study confirmed explant culture as a suitable model for analyzing inflammatory regulation in the bovine endometrium and identified IL10 as a potent modulator of endometrial IL1B and IL1RA. IMPLICATIONS: Not only Escherichia coli and Bacillus pumilus, but also unexpectedly Lactobacillus buchneri, induced initial pro-inflammatory mRNA expression patterns to be different depending on culture duration. This highlights the need for further research on pathogenic and opportunistic versus potentially protective bacteria in bovine uterine health.
